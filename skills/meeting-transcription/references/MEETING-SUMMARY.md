# Meeting Summary Reference

## Recommended Output

- Context: meeting title, date, participants, source.
- Executive summary: 3-6 bullets.
- Decisions: exact decisions and evidence.
- Action items: owner, task, due date, confidence.
- Blockers: blocker, affected work, unblock condition.
- Open questions: unresolved items.
- Follow-ups: reminders, docs, messages, or next meeting.

## Evidence Rules

Use "unknown" instead of guessing owner, date, or decision status. Preserve transcript snippets only when short and necessary.
