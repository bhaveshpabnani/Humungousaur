# Activity Skill Pack: Presentation Creation

## Summary

Use when the user is outlining, designing, editing, rehearsing, exporting, or
sharing a slide-based presentation. Optimize for narrative continuity, asset
tracking, and delivery readiness.

## Signals

- Slide creation, layout edits, speaker notes, image/media insertion, chart
  updates, theme changes, export, rehearsal, or presentation mode.
- Switching among source material, images, charts, documents, meeting notes, and
  slides.
- Repeated edits to sequence, titles, visuals, talking points, or delivery assets.

## Helpful Moments

- The user returns to an unfinished deck and may need a narrative resume capsule.
- Slides appear ready for review, export, rehearsal, or meeting follow-up.
- Missing assets, inconsistent structure, or repeated slide reordering suggests a
  blocker.
- The user explicitly asks for story, design, speaker notes, visuals, or polish.

## Stay Silent When

- The deck is only opened briefly, previewed, or background-synced.
- Assistance would require reading slide text, notes, images, source files, or
  meeting context without approval.
- The deck appears confidential, client-sensitive, personal, or policy-restricted.
- The user is presenting live unless explicitly invoked.

## Deep Dive Triggers

- Reading slide contents, speaker notes, media, charts, comments, source files, or
  linked meeting material.
- Rewriting narrative, creating speaker notes, auditing design, or generating
  presentation assets.
- Exporting, sharing, sending, or presenting on the user's behalf.

## Memory Guidance

- Store redacted deck/entity hashes, broad audience or purpose if safely known,
  source/asset counts, stage of work, last safe action, and export/share state.
- Remember user-stated narrative goals, delivery constraints, and review outcomes.
- Do not retain slide text, notes, images, names, file titles, or source content
  without approval.

## Privacy Notes

- Treat decks as potentially confidential external-facing artifacts.
- Ask before inspecting slide or asset content.
- Stay silent during live presentation unless the user asks for help.
