# Activity Guide: Design Review

## Summary
Use this guide when activity suggests the user is reviewing, commenting on, or exporting design work in tools such as Figma, FigJam, Miro, Canva, Sketch, or presentation/design surfaces.

## Signals
- Design file, board, frame, prototype, comment, export, or presentation metadata repeats across recent events.
- The same design entity appears with review, comment, prototype, version, or export actions.
- Design activity is paired with product docs, issue trackers, or meeting follow-up.

## Helpful Moments
- Prepare a quiet critique checklist or summary at a safe boundary.
- Offer to turn comments into issues, tasks, or a review memo after user approval.
- Ask only when the user appears blocked or explicitly invites assistance.

## Stay Silent When
- The activity is only background sync, preview, or navigation.
- The design surface is private, muted, or lacks enough continuity.
- The user is presenting live and interruption would be disruptive.

## Deep Dive Triggers
- Reading exact comments, frame names, screenshots, or prototype content requires approval.
- Exporting, posting comments, or modifying a design requires explicit approval.

## Memory Guidance
- Remember stable project/design entity refs and safe progress summaries only after helpful feedback.
- Do not remember raw design text, comments, screenshots, customer names, or visual content by default.

## Privacy Notes
- Treat design titles, comments, screenshots, and participant names as sensitive.
- Use hashed file/board/frame refs and redacted action labels by default.
