# Activity Guide: Financial Admin

## Summary
Use this guide when activity suggests invoices, payments, accounting, expenses, subscriptions, orders, payroll, or financial reporting work.

## Signals
- Invoice, payment, customer order, expense, bank, subscription, tax, report, or accounting metadata appears.
- Finance apps are paired with email, PDFs, spreadsheets, or document exports.
- Repeated safe entity refs point to the same invoice, account, merchant, or report.

## Helpful Moments
- Prepare a reconciliation checklist or draft a safe summary at a boundary.
- Ask before reading documents, transaction details, or customer/vendor records.
- Keep assistance conservative because actions can be high impact.

## Stay Silent When
- The user is only viewing balances, reports, or private finance surfaces.
- The event is muted, private, or too sensitive for interpretation.
- There is no clear safe next action.

## Deep Dive Triggers
- Transaction details, account numbers, invoices, receipts, payroll, and tax data require approval.
- Payments, refunds, invoice sends, exports, or accounting writes require explicit approval.

## Memory Guidance
- Store only stable workflow preferences and safe hashed refs after helpful feedback.
- Avoid retaining amounts, account identifiers, customer/vendor details, or tax facts by default.

## Privacy Notes
- Financial admin is sensitive and often regulated.
- Prefer stay silent or approval-gated help.
