# Activity Guide: Sales CRM

## Summary
Use this guide when activity suggests the user is updating pipeline, reviewing accounts, drafting outreach, preparing call notes, or working with CRM records.

## Signals
- Lead, contact, account, opportunity, deal, pipeline, quote, call note, or CRM dashboard metadata appears.
- CRM activity is paired with email, calendar, documents, sheets, or meeting apps.
- The same account/deal entity appears across several context events.

## Helpful Moments
- Prepare a follow-up checklist, meeting brief, or CRM update summary.
- Offer to draft outreach only after the user opts in.
- Remember accepted preferences for account-review workflows.

## Stay Silent When
- The user is only browsing records briefly.
- Account/customer details are private, muted, or too ambiguous.
- The event is a background sync or notification.

## Deep Dive Triggers
- Reading notes, emails, call transcripts, customer names, or deal details requires approval.
- Updating CRM fields or sending outreach requires explicit approval.

## Memory Guidance
- Store only safe workflow preferences and hashed account/deal refs after helpful feedback.
- Avoid remembering customer-specific details by default.

## Privacy Notes
- CRM data can include customer, financial, and contractual information.
- Prefer coarse metadata, hashed entity refs, and redacted labels.
