# Code of Conduct

Humungousaur should be a serious, welcoming place to build local-first agent technology.

## Our Standards

We expect contributors to:

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent while still asking for evidence
- keep criticism focused on code, docs, safety, tests, and user impact
- disclose AI-assisted contributions when relevant
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, sexualized language, or discriminatory behavior
- avoid publishing private data, secrets, or exploit details in public threads

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or discussions that violate these standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Security-sensitive behavior, including credential exposure, exploit publication without coordination, or attempts to bypass approval gates against another person's environment, should be handled through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers through a private channel. Include links, screenshots, timestamps, and context where possible.
